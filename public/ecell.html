Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell)

Delhi Technical Campus, Greater Noida

About E-Cell

The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) at Delhi Technical Campus is a dynamic platform that nurtures entrepreneurial spirit and innovation among students. It aims to create a thriving startup ecosystem by providing mentorship, guidance, and resources to aspiring student entrepreneurs.

Vision & Mission

  • Vision: To ignite the flame of entrepreneurship and innovation in every student.
  • Mission: Empower students with entrepreneurial knowledge, mentorship, and real-world exposure to build scalable startups and drive social impact.

Activities & Workshops

  • Startup Bootcamps and Ideation Hackathons
  • Guest talks by industry leaders and successful founders
  • Pitching competitions and Business Plan contests
  • Workshops on Design Thinking, MVP building, and Product-Market Fit
  • Investor Meetups and Demo Days

Our Achievements

  • 20+ startups incubated through E-Cell student community
  • Winners of Inter-college Entrepreneurship Challenge 2023
  • Collaborations with leading incubators and accelerators

How to Join E-Cell?

Passionate about startups? Want to build your own venture? Join the E-Cell by filling out the online application form available on the DTC student portal. Regular recruitment drives are conducted at the beginning of each semester.

You can also follow us on our Instagram and LinkedIn pages for latest updates.
