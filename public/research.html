Research Department – Delhi Technical Campus

About the Research Department

The Research Department at Delhi Technical Campus plays a pivotal role in fostering a culture of innovation, critical thinking, and academic inquiry. We aim to promote interdisciplinary research and provide a platform for students and faculty to engage in cutting-edge developments across various domains.

Objectives

  • To encourage a strong research culture among faculty and students.
  • To collaborate with industries and other academic institutions for joint research projects.
  • To publish high-quality research papers and file patents.
  • To conduct seminars, workshops, and conferences on emerging technologies.

Areas of Research

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Data Science & Big Data Analytics
  • Sustainable Architecture and Design
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Renewable Energy & Environmental Engineering

Collaborations

The department has tie-ups and collaborations with reputed institutions and industries like IIT Delhi, DRDO, TCS, and Microsoft Innovation Center for various research initiatives.

Recent Achievements

  • Over 50+ publications in reputed national and international journals in the past year.
  • Secured research grants from AICTE and DST.
  • Faculty filed multiple patents in the domains of AI, Robotics, and Architecture.
