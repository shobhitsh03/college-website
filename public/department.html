Departments at Delhi Technical Campus

Greater Noida

B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology)

The B.Tech program is the flagship engineering course at DTC, offering cutting-edge knowledge in CSE, AI, ML, Data Science, and more. With modern labs, experienced faculty, and strong industry collaborations, the program prepares students for both technical careers and innovation-driven entrepreneurship.

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)

The BCA program focuses on the fundamentals of programming, databases, web development, and software engineering. It’s ideal for students looking to build careers in software development, IT services, or further specialize in areas like app development and cybersecurity.

B.Arch (Bachelor of Architecture)

The B.Arch department nurtures creativity and critical thinking in the field of architecture and design. Students engage with real-world design challenges, sustainable practices, and architectural software to prepare for a career in architecture and urban planning.

B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce)

The B.Com program covers core areas like accounting, finance, business law, and taxation. It’s a great foundation for careers in banking, commerce, auditing, or preparing for competitive exams like CA, CS, and MBA.
